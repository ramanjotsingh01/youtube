Our Mission

At Reduce Food Waste, we are committed to fighting hunger and reducing food waste in our communities. Our mission is to rescue surplus food and distribute it to those in need, helping to alleviate hunger and promote sustainability.

Why Choose Us?

  1. Impactful Initiatives: We run impactful initiatives to rescue surplus food from restaurants, events, and markets.
  2. Community Engagement: Join a passionate community dedicated to making a difference in the fight against hunger.
  3. Sustainability: By reducing food waste, we're not just feeding people in need; we're also protecting the environment.
  4. Transparency: We believe in transparency and accountability in everything we do. See the impact of your contributions firsthand.

Our Services

  • Surplus Food Collection
  • Food Distribution
  • Community Outreach Programs
  • Educational Workshops
  • Advocacy and Policy Initiatives
